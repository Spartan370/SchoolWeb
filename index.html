Our Mascot

The Mighty Gladiator

Our mascot is a Gladiator, representing strength, honor, and the fighting spirit of our university. Dressed in Roman armor with our colors, maroon and navy, the Gladiator shows courage and determination. At sports events, it pumps up the crowd with sword tricks and shield-pounding, which has become a tradition.
